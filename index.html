Today's Highlight

Experience Tokyo Like a Local

Start your day with a visit to Senso-ji Temple, Tokyo's most famous Buddhist temple. Wander through Nakamise shopping street for traditional snacks before experiencing the bustling Shibuya Crossing - the world's busiest pedestrian intersection.

For lunch, try authentic ramen at a tiny local shop, then participate in a traditional tea ceremony in the afternoon. End your day at an izakaya (Japanese pub) with locals, trying small plates and sake.
