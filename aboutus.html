Our Story

How a shared love for cultural exploration became a global platform

Born From a Passion for Connection

World Culture Roulette began in 2018 when our founders, a group of travel enthusiasts and cultural researchers, found themselves frustrated by the limitations of physical travel. They realized that while not everyone can visit every country, everyone deserves to experience the richness of global cultures.

What started as a small passion project with a handful of destination guides has grown into a comprehensive platform with daily cultural experiences from over 150 countries. Our team now includes anthropologists, local experts, and passionate storytellers from around the world.

We believe that cultural understanding fosters global connection. Whether you're a frequent traveler, an armchair explorer, or an educator looking to broaden your students' horizons, we're here to make the world feel a little smaller and a lot more connected.
