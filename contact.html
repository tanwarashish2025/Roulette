Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about World Culture Roulette

How do I suggest a destination or cultural experience?

We love hearing suggestions from our community! You can email us at suggestions@worldcultureroulette.com with details about the destination or cultural experience you'd like to see featured. Please include as much information as possible about why this would be valuable for our community.

Can I contribute content to World Culture Roulette?

Yes! We welcome contributions from cultural experts, travel writers, and local guides. Please send us samples of your work along with a brief introduction about your expertise to contributors@worldcultureroulette.com. Our editorial team will review your submission and get back to you within 2-3 weeks.

How often is the daily cultural experience updated?

Our daily cultural experience updates at midnight PST each day. If you're subscribed to our newsletter, you'll receive the new experience in your inbox by 6am PST. You can also always check back on our website or app for the latest cultural journey.

Do you offer educational discounts?

We offer special pricing for educators and institutions. Please contact our education team at education@worldcultureroulette.com with details about your school or organization, and we'll provide information about our educational packages and discounts.

How can I report an error in cultural information?

Cultural accuracy is extremely important to us. If you've noticed any inaccuracies in our content, please email corrections@worldcultureroulette.com with the specific details and sources that verify the correct information. Our research team will investigate and make any necessary updates.
